How it works

A calm, clear process.

  1. 1

    Talk

    A 15-minute call or a few texts. You tell me what you're trying to do; I tell you honestly what I'd do in your shoes.

  2. 2

    Plan

    Budget, timeline, financing, and strategy on paper. Buyers get a search plan; sellers get comps, prep, and likely net.

  3. 3

    Execute

    Showings, offers, listing, negotiation. You'll always know what's happening and what's next — no radio silence.

  4. 4

    Close & beyond

    Inspection to keys, and I stay useful after closing: values, refis, rentals, and the next move when it comes.
