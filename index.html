About Rimyan

I work with first-time buyers navigating a competitive market, homeowners looking to sell at the right price, and investors building portfolios across the Front Range. Whether it's a single-family home in Briargate, a duplex in Arvada, or land in El Paso County, you get market data, honest advice, and a commitment to getting the deal right.

PCS Partners Military Real Estate

Moving on military orders?

A PCS move runs on a tight clock and a lot of unknowns. I help service members and their families land in the right home and the right neighborhood — whether you're reporting to Fort Carson, Peterson, Schriever, the Air Force Academy, or anywhere along the Front Range.

  • Relocation planned around your report date and house-hunting trip
  • Virtual tours and remote paperwork when you're still out of state
  • Neighborhood, commute, and school guidance for each installation
  • Connections to lenders and vendors familiar with military buyers
