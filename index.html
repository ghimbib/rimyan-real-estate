About Rimyan

Rimyan is the real estate website and client resource for Bibek Ghimire, a Colorado real estate broker with PCS Partners Military Real Estate.

I work with first-time buyers navigating a competitive market, homeowners looking to sell at the right price, and investors building portfolios across the Front Range. Whether it's a single-family home in Briargate, a duplex in Arvada, or land in El Paso County, you get market data, honest advice, and a commitment to getting the deal right.

My approach combines real estate service with a technology-first mindset: comparable sales, market timing, negotiation leverage, and clear next steps instead of gut feelings. You get a strategy, not a sales pitch.
